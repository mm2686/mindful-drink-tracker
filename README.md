## ðŸ”’ Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: February 2026**

Your privacy is the core of this app. The Mindful Drink Tracker follows a "Privacy by Design" philosophy:

1. **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal drinking logs or personal information.
2. **Local Storage Only:** All data you enter is stored strictly on your own device's local database.
3. **No Third-Party Tracking:** There are no analytics, trackers, or advertisements included in the app.
4. **Transparency:** The app requires only the "Notifications" permission if you choose to set mindful reminders.

This app is provided as a tool for personal wellness and reflection.
